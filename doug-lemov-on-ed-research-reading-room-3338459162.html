Doug Lemov on Ed Research Reading Room

Ollie Lowell (a math educator from Melbourne) hosts a podcast in which he interviews influential education researchers. In this episode, he interviews Doug Lemov and gets Doug to provide concrete advice/examples for about 16 different instructional moves. He also does a bit to get a response to critiques that his approach is too controlling and does little to develop student independence and ability to excel in learning settings that are less teacher-led.
