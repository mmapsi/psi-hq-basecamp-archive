üëç New Resource: Archive of External Resources shared on Basecamp

Are you loving the amazing external articles and resources that are shared on Basecamp, but worried you won't be able to find them when you need them several weeks or months from now?

You're not alone! In response to the need surfaced by several implementers to have a way to easily locate and search through external resources that have been shared on the Basecamp HQ Message Board this school year, we started a very simple tracking system in the form of this External Resources Archive spreadsheet. Here's how it will work: 
  • I will update this spreadsheet twice per month during the 20-21 school year with links to any EXTERNAL resources that were shared the via Basecamp HQ Message Board (e.g. articles, research, etc) 
  • I will not include documents shared on Basecamp that have been created internally by PDS to support program (e.g. TOA Templates, CAF Summary, etc.) - those will be filed in Drive and are part of our larger program resource filing system.
  • We will use this archive in 20/21 for learning about a few knowledge management questions: What types of emergent, "in the moment" external resources we are sharing and seeking at Partners? What is the best way to vet and archive this type of external resource going forward so it is easy to access & integrated into our day-to-day workflow?
  • You can access the "External Resource Archive - Basecamp" spreadsheet anytime on the PSI Home Screen under "Program Resources". 
Let me know if you have any questions or suggestions for this process!
