WorkLife and Burnout

Adam Grant's podcast series on "how to make work not suck" posted an episode on Burnout - what it is and strategies for reducing it. Adam explores a model of reducing burnout with several guests who engage in this work. The model is:

Demand-Control-Support
  1. reduce the demands of the job, 
  2. give people more control to manage them, and 
  3. provide more support to help them cope.
