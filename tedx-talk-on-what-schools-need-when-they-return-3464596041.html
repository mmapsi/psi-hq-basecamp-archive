Thanks so much for sharing this, Alex. I believe that families are often excluded in black and brown school communities. I liked that the speaker mentioned family engagement as the first priority. This shifts the narrative from a deficit-based discussion i.e. "skill gap" to asset-based, and helps to learn about the lives of students and families outside of the classroom.

Lastly, rethinking assessments and the way students receive feedback vs our traditional grading systems which is sometimes rooted in bias and/or compliance. All great stuff. Thanks again.
