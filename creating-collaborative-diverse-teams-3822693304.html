From NSI COP (and this resource may already well be at play already in our Gates Networks):

A one pager that seems particularly timely since many of our sites are at the end of the year and determing team representation for 21-22. This resource hopefully will be useful with a couple of my sites that have had the same ILT members on their ILT's for years. Often times principals feel they must make the decisions about who should be on the team, often discouraging those with dissenting voices from participating. No judgment here, just a noticing. This resource could start a conversation with leaders about doing some reflection on the members of the team through a more equitable lens, starting with an individual reflection.
If for some reason the link doesn't work, check your inboxes today for an email titled "Pass It On #5: Supporting Students Through Transitions This Summer" from NSI Community of Practice.
