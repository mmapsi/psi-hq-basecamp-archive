Joe Soss on the Minnesota Paradox

A good friend of mine from grad school at UW-Madison is public policy prof at U of Minnesota's Humphrey School. His work has been centered on systems of oppression that create and perpetuate political, social, and economic inequities. This is a short piece that helps unpack what's been in the press a bunch recently around the Black and Native American vs white wealth and school outcome gaps that are so stark in Minnesota.
