I'm literally speechless and had to share (I'm rusty on Basecamp so I don't know if this is where I do that but I'm giving it a whirl). I just sat in on the Michelle Obama ES staff mtg (WCC) to hear all about their plans for next year, how they're interpreting their MOU, and how they are developing relationships with each other. They have been working non-stop all summer to prepare for this school year, and it shows! Under the direction of a fairly detailed plan from the district (a district that took a big budget cut this year, btw), M. Obama Elementary is well on its way to becoming a model school for distance learning, collaboration and leadership, not just in the district, but I would go so far as to say nationwide. I'm dropping the mic left and right!  Special shout-out to principal Claudia Velez, a real visionary leader.  
What a huge privilege it is to be in support of this awesomeness. I'm sitting in loads and loads of gratitude today. 
I hope to be a conduit of some of these great practices from Obama to our org in some way, and to learn from you all what's working. In truly unprecedented times, amidst three pandemics, teachers rise to EVERY OCCASION and MAKE THINGS HAPPEN for kids.
