SBAC in Cali?

Does anyone have any up to date information as to whether the SBAC will be administered in some way shape or form in Cali this year?

Comments & Events

Chris Thorn, Chief Program Officer
In December, I sat in on the California Practitioners Advisory Group Meeting instead of Derek. In that meeting, the CDE assessment team announced that they planned to administer a shortened version of the SBAC exams this spring. This would include a slightly longer administration window and an even longer makeup window.

What I heard in an Assessment for Learning meeting today from several folks who are plugged into policymaking in California, is that despite their earlier commitment to test, the Board of Education is likely to ask the US Dept of Ed for a waiver to not test this year. In the January meeting, they discussed the waiver from several aspects of school accountability (particularly CSI status) that was approved. The February Agenda is not yet posted, but I would not be surprised if there was at least a discussion to ask for a waiver to not administer the SBAC exam this year.
