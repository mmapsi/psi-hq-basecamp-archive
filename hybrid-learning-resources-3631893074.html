Hybrid Learning Resources

Hey everyone!  In our bay area WGL session earlier we were talking about how to support schools in the bay that are returning to in person learning in a couple of weeks.  I found this recent article helpful: https://www.edsurge.com/news/2021-03-17-juggling-roomers-and-zoomers-how-teachers-make-hybrid-learning-work

The recommendations are mostly good teacher and school culture practices, but they do a pretty good job helping to see why prioritizing things like a strong culture and fostering ownership is important.

Anyone else have resources that they've found useful?

I got this resource through the Transcend Design Community slack channel, which I recommend joining for a pretty consistent stream of good stuff.  https://www.transcendeducation.org/community-join
