Hi Everyone,

As I have mentioned to anyone who will listen, I think that the PDSA cycle (Plan, Do, Study, Act) is the same idea as ROCI. In the improvement science world, however, it has been used to test smaller scale changes more systematically than we sometimes do with our own ROCI cycle. In a pandemic year, where we are working alongside other improvement science organizations, it is the perfect moment to push ourselves to focus on testing out smaller scale changes and then leveraging that learning to scale change and plan annual theories of action based on tested changes! To that end the PDS team developed a tool: Using Cycle ROCI to Test Change Ideas in the Classroom (it includes a short adaptation for used with leaders to test learning systems). There is also an accompanying note taker(please make a copy). After your try these out, please leave your feedback!

Enjoy!
