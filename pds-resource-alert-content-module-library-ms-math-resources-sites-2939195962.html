Hey there, implementers! Here are 2 new resources to support your work with networks: 
  1. Planning your network Scope & Sequence and wondering where to access "known good" content modules? Start here! In order to minimize the time required for network leads to prepare content, as well as to ensure continuity across regions, we created the Content Modules Library. This is our go-to library for our most frequently used content. We will continue to add new modules & updates to these as we get feedback.
  2. To support middle grades math teachers, we have compiled a set of resources for each grade level (6th-8th) organized by the various strands in the CCSS Math standards on the Middle School Math Resources site. These resources are intended for use in network sessions to support teacher teams as they engage in standards-based ROCI cycles & to support individual teacher coaching. (Look for a content module to use when introducing the site with your network - coming soon!)
Let me know if you have questions or comments. Go team!
