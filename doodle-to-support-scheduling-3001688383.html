Tools to manage complexity
We are going to offer Doodle accounts to anyone who wants them. You may have used Doodle to create polls to find an available collective time to meet. That‚Äôs still a feature. A new feature this is likely to be valuable for anyone trying to schedule virtual meetings with school and district staff members is the BookMe link. You can tie your Doodle account to your Partners Google Calendar and people can see when you are free to book an appointment. There are lots of settings, but folks can see is times you are free to meet. An additional great feature is that you can also connect your Zoom account so that when someone books a meeting it automatically inserts the Zoom link as well. The whole thing can save lots of time and effort.
