Inspired Teaching Workshop - Making Schools Worth It

Did anyone else attend Inspired Teaching's "Making Schools Worth It" session that Cynthia shared with us? I did, and found it to be super valuable. There are some resources that I think we can integrate into our coaching or network sessions as we support teams to plan for return to in-person and opening up schools...The session really centered students and I really appreciated that. 

We spent the session collecting and analyzing information about our students‚Äô and our own experiences this past year. We looked at what we‚Äôve lost, and also at how we‚Äôve grown and what we‚Äôve learned. We then used these insights/data to engage in a design thinking exercise. For the "empathize" phase of design thinking we watched three videos of an elementary, middle, and high school student offering advice to teachers, and sharing ideas for making school engaging right now. We then used data from the student voices to "define a problem" and finally we "ideated" by leveraging our creativity to imagine a wide array of solutions.
