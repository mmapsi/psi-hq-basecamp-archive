The Leadership Academy has some helpful publications. The one attached is divided into 8 areas, followed by indicators and reflection questions after each one. I could see using some of the questions during STR focal groups or leadership interviews. The indicators are great as well, and could be used as a tool for site teams during an equity walk or something similar. It's a very accessible and readable resource. Don't be put off by the number of pages.

Samples:
Area 1: Equity and Access
Indicator:
(1) All staff members are familiar with the varied demographic groups in the school, the neighborhoods students live in, and the cultural and linguistic traditions each possesses.
(2) Remote and in-person school assemblies, special programs, and speakers reflect the diverse nature of the school and larger community and/or bring a diverse set of ideas and experiences based on gender, race, ethnicity, disability and ability.
Reflection question: What opportunities do we provide for students to authentically engage with members of the community who come from different cultures and backgrounds than themselves?
