Hello all,
For those of you who participated in sharing and reflecting on change agent stories during Dec. 4th WGL, we are following up to share some highlights and trends from your stories. As someone who just joined Jan 2021, I appreciate this data sharing. I want to read more of the individual stories submitted, but so far, most salient to me is the words used most - hopeful, energized, and focused. We can channel this energy during our work this year.

However, as shown in Slide 10, how do we encourage individual action to become more collective efficacy? It was comforting to see that overarching strategy is apparent, but it is also warranted to address the urgent needs felt by those on the far right of the spectrum on Slide 11. Personally, I am working on learning from others rather than coaching others - a perspective that I've greatly appreciated during the last three weeks. Slide 14 feels so Below the Green Line to me - how are we moving towards relationships, rather than structures and processes?
