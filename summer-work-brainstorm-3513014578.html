Summer Work Brainstorm

Hi All,

We are currently working to determine our 2021-2022 Program priorities that will drive the focus of our summer work. We are also working to refine our summer work design principles. We will be finalizing the scope and options for summer work in May.

For anyone new to the org, generally, summer is a time to take PTO and recharge, as well as to collaborate with colleagues to learn new things and/ or contribute to program development priorities (like revising rubrics, developing new content modules, developing materials or resources we need and don't have).

As we (Cynthia, Chris, Amanda, Rahwa, Mercy, and Kim) dive into thinking about summer, we wanted to make sure you all were in on the idea brainstorming about how to spend our time. Please feel free to plop ideas here as they come to you! Or add details and thoughts to existing ideas. We will narrow later -- this is generative. 

Enjoy!
