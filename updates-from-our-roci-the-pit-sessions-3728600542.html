Hi all, 

Big thanks to folks who took the time to give feedback on how the PIT is working for you. The PIT refinement group has reviewed all of the feedback and here's what's coming up on top.

We will do our best to:
  1. Continue providing space in learning systems for calibration conversations and additional support/guidance on how-to leverage the PIT. 
  2. Connect the PIT with strategy docs and other Partners frameworks where possible. 
  3. Complete an equity audit of the PIT as a first step to better incorporate RCCP and equity as a through line.
  4. Provide clarity on strong start line items and goals for PIT completion.
  5. Adjust the status options, specifically replace "Needs Attention"
  6. Improve usability by eliminating the need to re-enter staff and coachee names across sheets and removing the automated filter reset. 
Our goal is to get team trackers set up in July/August this year. Thanks again for sharing your improvement ideas!
