Two New Resources: RCCP and Improvement Science

In continuing to highlight new resources, we wanted to share a pair of new one-pagers that have been developed and leveraged in various contexts this year.

Talk Frames for Courageous Conversations- this resource came out of an equity series at Edenvale.  It has sentence starters/frames for engaging in courageous conversations aligned to Glen Singleton's 4 agreements for courageous conversations and can be leveraged with partners who are looking to develop their muscle in engaging in equity conversations.

ROCI and Model for Improvement/PDSA Crosswalk - this resource came from a request from a Philly principal who has training on PDSA implementation and wanted to see how it connects to our ROCI cycle.  It continues the work that we have been doing to understand how our approach to equity-focused continuous improvement connects to how other organizations implement improvement science. 

Hoping these are relevant and useful in your work.  If anyone tries them out we'd love to hear feedback!
