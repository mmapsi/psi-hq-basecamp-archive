Attendance & Engagement During Covid-19: New Resource

Hi all,

Based on the positive feedback on the equitable grading overview, we created a parallel overview of best practices for attendance and engagement during Covid-19.  It includes a summary of the research on emergent best practices for monitoring attendance/engagement during remote learning as well as a suggested approach for schools to being engaging in ROCI around their current attendance/engagement. Please share feedback, additional resources on the topic, etc. and keep the resource requests coming!

-Jesse
