Hey all,

I wanted to share the resources that I introduced in Data Dive today.

PIT Calibration Log: This is the place to document our ongoing calibration conversations. When you have a question about an item, please send it to support@partnersinschools.org. You'll get a response in email and we will record the questions and answers in the log. If you have a calibration conversation in a team meeting, please enter the summary of that conversation in the log.

Emergent Learning Data Protocol: This is the protocol that we used in November Data Dive when reflecting on PIT data. Please make a copy of the template to use in your team meetings. Remember you can invite me or Mallory to facilitate for you.

Framing Research Questions: A useful post about applying an equity lens to craft questions that reflect who is expected to change. Focus is on changing the environment to create conditions for success.

Thanks all,
