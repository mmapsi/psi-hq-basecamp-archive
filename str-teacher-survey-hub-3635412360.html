Mercy Sanchez, Director of Data Evaluation
Hello everyone, 

Wanted to shareout an interesting anecdote of how my calibration team balanced data from the STR survey and focus groups--

When scoring line item 2.1.07 Examination of Race, Culture, Class, and Power the team reviewed the STR Survey and found teachers overwhelmingly agreed (close to 90% and one of the highest-rated survey items) that they seek to examine RCCP in their work. However, during focus groups what surfaced was that teachers largely consider examining RCCP to be something that happens because it is embedded in the curriculum. Moreover, there were not a lot of conversations about their own reflections around RCCP. Anyhow, I thought this was a useful insight on how two pieces of data can be used together to deepen our understanding of the school and wanted to pass it along. ‚ò∫Ô∏è
