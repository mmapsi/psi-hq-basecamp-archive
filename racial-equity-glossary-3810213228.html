Chris Thorn, Chief Program Officer
Exploring and re-exploring terms/how groups talk about themselves is one of the reasons I think NPR's Code Switch is such an engaging and thoughtful podcast. Last September, in the episode, Is it time to say RIP to POC, they dug into the term BIPOC and talked to lots of people who have very clear ideas about the terminology they prefer. In the episode from May 18, 2021, The Sum of Our Parts, they revisit the notion of simplistic labels that can lead many folks to feel that they are not being seen. 

One of the challenges of any "definitive" set of definitions is that our understanding changes over time as does the social use of language. There is no immutable definitions or term that will stand the test of time.
