Emily Becker, Transformation Network Partner
PS: Does everyone know about the playback speed tool on YouTube? In Settings (bottom right hand corner), click on "Playback Speed," and adjust as you wish. I found this setting incredibly helpful when working with ELs and/or students who need to hear something more slowly. I haven't ever seen the need to speed up the playback speed, but that is an option.
