I wanted to highlight the plan that one school used after getting their FIRST EVER STR Summary....

August Boeger (Team ESA School in San Jose) leaders decided to share the Themes overview with their staff in a staff meeting. They gave teachers time to read and then fill in a jamboard that had two frames "Positive Themes" and "Areas to Focus Next Year".

Following that staff meeting the leaders shared a Google Form that said "Please choose the top 3 areas where you think we should prioritize for next year" that came from the themes identified in the report and on the jamboard!

It's going to be a great starting point from my Renew conversations with those leaders!

Reach out if you want to talk about how the plan came about or want to look at what they created!
