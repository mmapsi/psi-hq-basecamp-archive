Support of English Learners (ELs)

This morning, I attended a webinar hosted by MPI (Migration Policy Institute) called Centering English Learners in Schools‚Äô Responses to the COVID-19 Pandemic. Especially for our team in CA, I wanted to share a few resources/tools discussed that could enhance network design and our coaching of school/district leaders to intentionally focus on supports for ELs. 

Here are a few resources that I wanted to highlight:
  • From Californians Together - this is a Distance Learning Reflection Tool which outlines best practices for ELs in distance learning. The tool is aligned to principles from the CA EL Roadmap
  • The Californians Together website provides other resources for EL support -- including LCAP planning
  • This policy brief includes 4 key policy recommendations for districts and schools to help them develop reopening/phase 2 plans that will meet the needs of ELs - could be helpful for our coaching of district leaders as this is at the forefront of conversations. 
  • Click here to find links to the Elementary, Middle, and High school versions of the California English Learner Roadmap teacher toolkit for your reference. On this webpage, there's also an administrators guide to help leaders engage teachers in the EL Roadmap.
