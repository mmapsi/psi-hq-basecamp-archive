Hey Alex ! The School District of Philadelphia uses Charlotte Danielson's Framework for Teaching for observations & feedback. Domain 1b is geared towards Knowledge of Students and expressly states things like culture, cultural heritage etc. for the purposes of planning and preparation. I am also linking a back-to-school letter that a leader sent home to families that openly expresses things like racial equity work, and racial justice. Not necessarily an essential teaching practice, but a way to think about using the language. I am gaining experience with this tool to think about culturally responsive teaching, and incorporating the language into vision statements. Good luck!
