Happy Monday all!

I've gotten feedback that folks appreciate the external resources that we have explored during our monthly data dives so I wanted to share a few of those resources here for easy reference and broader reach!
  1. Data Equity Framework - A seven step framework from We All Count hat offers a systematic way of looking at data projects and surfacing equity issues. 
  2. Dear Data - For funsies, a collection of hand drawn, personal data visualizations that remind us of the potential to use data in a humanizing way that promotes connection!
  3. Data Visualization Catalogue and Storytelling with Data - Two guides to help us identify more options for visually communicating with data and identifying which charts to to use.
