The Intersection of Race & Disability with Imani Barbarin

Attended a great virtual event last weekend and thought to pass along the video recording. The topic of the conversation was: The Intersection of Race & Disability with Imani Barbarin. The event was organized by H-CAN (Havertown Area Community Action Network) here in PA. 

Imani, a popular blogger & social activist, powerfully addressed: intersectionality, growing disability amidst a pandemic, access & ableism, anti-racism, white privilege, and the events at the Capitol. I know that many of us have been looking for ways to approach the topics on access, whiteness, anti-racism, and the events at the Capitol. Check it out! https://www.facebook.com/HavertownAreaCommunityActionNetwork/
