Morning Philadelphia and ESA Network Teams!

I think many of you have heard expectations regarding the end of year NERF and PNI Survey in various spaces, and I wanted to also mirror that message here for reference: 
  • The PNI Survey is the main End-of-Year deliverable for the Gates Network, so please focus your time/efforts ensuring that is administered and returned
  • Please do not administer a session NERF on the day you administer the PNI Survey
  • The annual section on the NERF is optional. However, please give some thought before choosing to add it when you administer your last session NERF; I'm all for more data (trust me), but between March and July, you'll have multiple data points for reference-- STR Teacher Survey, STR visits, monthly NERF data, PNI Survey, and what you know from working at your schools/networks. Please reach out to me if you will need an annual NERF section added to your NERF.
Last, reminder there's no EOY Client Satisfaction Survey for Gates.

I'm also re-attaching the NERF, How-To Guide since there have been questions about the customization of the objectives.
