The UK's Education Endowment Foundation (EFF) does a consistently better job packaging research results for educators than does the U.S. Department of Education (Edutopia and Digital Promise are probably the best at this in the U.S.) This latest EFF report provides engagement advice in 4 areas - 
  1. Critically review how you work with parents
  2. Provide practical strategies to support learning at home
  3. Tailor school communications to encourage positive dialogue about learning
  4. Offer more sustained and intensive support where needed
There's both an easy-to-read summary and a longer report that goes into more depth around the research supporting each set of strategies.
