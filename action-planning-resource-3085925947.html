Hi Everyone!

One of the coaching line items in the Program Implementation Tracker is about supporting your site based teams to create a professional learning plan or action plan. That line item is referencing a 30 day or cycle action plan that you might use when our stage of support and level of support does not lend to developing a full PLP. Jesse put together an action planning resource that lays out pre-requisites for action planning, as well as multiple types of action planning you might engage in. Linked in the overview is a cycle or 30 day action plan template.

In the document there is a link to some PDSA planning tools that I share with some caution. I think this is a great moment for us as an organization to interrogate and tighten up our own approach as we learn from other Gates grantees. As we work in the field, however we can't increase the burden on our clients to make sense of different language used to say the same thing (PDSA versus ROCI). The more we can link all of our support back to ROCI and equity the more streamlined it will feel for our clients. On the PDS side, we are learning alongside you all and trying to develop resources as they are needed that will support you to integrate our improvement science learnings into our existing resources. Reach out to me if you have one you need help thinking through!
